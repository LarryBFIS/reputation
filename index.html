01 · The reply pipeline

Replies in your voice

Every review gets drafted, tiered by risk, and queued for the right person. Low-risk replies post in 4 hours. High-risk reviews go straight to the GM. No "thanks for your feedback" energy.

02 · The request pipeline

Special requests, closed-loop

Forward your special-request inbox once. We extract intent, push to your GM on WhatsApp with a 1-tap approve, schedule a 9 AM front-desk reminder, and check post-stay if it landed.

03 · The analytics pipeline
